About Me

Hi there, and welcome!

My name is Sochi — I’m a 21-year-old graduate of Red River College Polytechnic, where I earned my diploma in Business Information Technology. I’m currently based in Winnipeg, Manitoba, though I proudly trace my roots back to Nigeria.

I’m a naturally curious and creative individual who thrives on exploring new ideas and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. I believe in dreaming big (and I mean really big!) because with the right mindset and a willing spirit, I truly believe anything can be achieved.

Throughout my time at college, I had the opportunity to work with a wide range of programming languages and tools. These experiences helped me build a number of projects—some tied to coursework, others purely driven by personal passion. Each one taught me something new and deepened my love for technology and problem-solving.

Thanks for stopping by—I’m excited to share a bit of my journey with you! Take a look at my projects and skills below for some insight into what I can do.

My Approach

My design style leans towards modern with a *slightly* minimalistic edge—but I never box myself into one aesthetic. I genuinely enjoy exploring a wide range of design approaches, and I find joy in dabbling across different styles to keep things fresh, dynamic, and exciting.

I'm not afraid to take bold risks with my designs. I believe experimentation is at the heart of creativity—you won’t know if something truly works until you try it. Through this process, I’m constantly refining my instincts and learning what resonates.

When designing for clients, I always start by listening carefully to their vision. From there, I imagine an elevated, more refined version of what they’re asking for—something that still aligns with their needs but feels fresh, intentional, and thoughtfully crafted. That vision becomes my north star throughout the project.
